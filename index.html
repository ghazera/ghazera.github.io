About

What is Scape?

Scape is a cooperative horror game for up to 4 players, the goal of which is to escape from a map.

But it's not that easy since a monster will come to ruin your plans and at the same time, do everything to exterminate you.

Face a hostile and scary environment with your friends.



Where can I find the game?

At the moment you cannot find it. The game is still in the prototype phase and it will take a long time to reach a stable version of the game. The game will be available on Steam from its Alpha version for 15$.



What is GameDia?

GameDia is an independent "group" of video game developers bringing together two developers:

- GHAZERA
- Saiko

It is also the two of us who are responsible for the development of Scape.
